About EndoSense

We’re building tools that put knowledge and control in women’s hands—helping each person navigate menopause with clarity, confidence, and care.

Technology

The EndoSense continuous hormone monitor (CHM) uses a robust recognition technology, capable of being regenerated by a small electrical current. This overcomes challenges with stability, regeneration and individual calibration that aptamer-based technologies face.
